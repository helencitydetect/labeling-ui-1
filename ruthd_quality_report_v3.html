Section 4

Reviewer Comment

Your exact match rate of 42% is close to the team average, and your work is generally solid on structural property elements. However, the reviewed sample reveals a consistent pattern of missing lawn condition attributes. Leaves, debris, and dead patches appear to be under-inspected — particularly on images that already have multiple structural labels. These three lawn attributes together account for nearly a third of all corrections in the sample.

A secondary pattern is over-labeling of paint and rust conditions on doors, walls, and porches. Attributes ending in _extreme require clearly visible and significant damage. Minor discoloration or surface wear does not qualify, and applying these labels on borderline cases adds review burden without improving annotation quality.

One actionable suggestion: before submitting each image, do a brief final check specifically on the lawn zone — leaves, debris, dead patches, grass height — as a separate step. This single habit should reduce the majority of missed labels seen in this sample.
